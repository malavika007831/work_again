====================================
PROJECT DESCRIPTION
====================================
Work Again is an AI-powered Career Re-Entry Ecosystem designed for professionals
who are returning to work after a career break due to caregiving, health,
relocation, layoffs, or personal growth, confidence boosting

The platform focuses on reducing resume gap bias, rebuilding confidence,
upskilling users, and connecting them with return-friendly job opportunities.
Work Again reframes career breaks as valuable life experiences rather than
weaknesses, helping skilled professionals re-enter the workforce successfully.

FEATURES LIST
====================================
1. Mentor Support  
   Guidance from experienced professionals.

2. Skill Development  
   Upskilling aligned with current job market needs.

3. Career Break Translator  
   Converts career gaps into professional strengths.

4. Industry Shift Predictor  
   Suggests alternative and adjacent career paths.

5. Career Analytics  
   Data-driven insights for successful re-entry.

6. Returnship Marketplace  
   Trial jobs, internships, and flexible roles.

7. Anonymous Community  
   Safe peer and mentor interaction space.
